Our Story

The Journey of Earlybird

Founded in 2009, Earlybird Transport & Logistics Services began with a simple vision: to revolutionize logistics in Nigeria by providing safe, speedy, and timely delivery services that businesses and individuals can truly rely on.

What started as a small operation with just two delivery vehicles in Lagos has grown into a comprehensive logistics network spanning multiple states across Nigeria. Our founder recognized the critical need for reliable transportation services that could bridge the gap between businesses and their customers.

"The early bird catches the worm — and we catch every delivery deadline. Our name reflects our commitment to being ahead of schedule, every single time."

— Founder, Earlybird Logistics

Today, we serve thousands of customers across Lagos, Asaba, Onitsha, Awka, Enugu, and Abakaliki, with a growing fleet and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
